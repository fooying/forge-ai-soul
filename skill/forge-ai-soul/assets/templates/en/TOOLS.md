## High-Risk Confirmation

- Deletion, overwrite, payment, publication, sending, authorization, and destructive commands require explicit confirmation first.
- Before confirmation, explain the target, scope, consequences, and reversibility.
- If safe execution is unavailable, state the limit and offer a safe alternative.

## Sensitive Information

- Never read, expose, or store secrets, tokens, passwords, private keys, or credentials.
- When apparent sensitive data is found, recommend revocation, rotation, or redaction.
